Module 1

🪟 The Johari Window

Self-awareness is the realization of concepts and situations, serving as the baseline for all leadership. We use the Johari Window to map our known and hidden traits.

Open Self

Known to self and others.
e.g., My technical skills

Blind Self

Not known to self, but known to others.

Hidden Self

Known to self, but hidden from others.

Unknown Self

Undiscovered potential.

Module 2

⚡ Drivers of Motivation

Motivation is a leader's primary function. It lines up with individual strengths and requires clear communication.

  • 🎯
    Herzberg's Theory:

    Balancing intrinsic satisfaction against extrinsic rewards.

  • 🏆
    McClelland's Needs:

    Professionals are driven by Achievement, Authority, or Affiliation.

  • 💡
    Leadership Insight:

    Fear is negative and exhausting; true motivation comes from inside each person.

Module 3

🎯 Vision & Mission

The Mission

Defines your purpose and what you are doing today.

"To deliver excellent service by ensuring prompt assistance and technical innovation."

The Vision

Extends your mission to an ideal future state.

"To become a standout leader in the IT-BPM sector, bridging technology and human connection."
