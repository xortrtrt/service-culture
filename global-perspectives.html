Modules 9, 10 & 11: Master Cultural Diversity Across Global Markets.

🌍 Service excellence requires cultural sensitivity. As IT-BPM professionals, we must adapt our service delivery to match the distinct standards of global markets.

🇺̧🇸 North America

Module 9

The ACSI Benchmark

The American Customer Satisfaction Index (ACSI) is a vital economic indicator in the US. Service here requires a direct, efficient approach focused heavily on measurable satisfaction rates and diversity enablers.

🇪̧🇺 Europe

Module 10

CEN Standards

The European Committee for Standardization (CEN) drives expectations. Over 84% of organizations utilize these strict national and European standards to guarantee high-quality service consistency and regulatory compliance.

🌏 Asia

Module 11

Cultural Adaptation

Asia represents a massively diverse consumer market. Western multinationals must adapt their standard behavioral models to align with local psychology, prioritizing high-context communication and deep cultural respect.
