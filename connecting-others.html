Module 4

💚 The Power of Empathy

Empathy is the capacity to recognize and share the feelings experienced by another person. It is critical for defusing conflict and building trust.

  • ✓ Helps navigate the "Social Radar"
  • ✓ Essential for understanding client needs
  • ✓ Defeats Empathy Blockers like defensiveness
"Empathy is placing one's self in the shoes of another."
Modules 5 & 6

🌱 Social Skills & The GROW Model

Soft skills are just as vital as hard technical skills. When managing teams, conflicts are inevitable. We use the GROW Coaching Model to turn challenges into learning opportunities.

G

Goal

Establish the objective of the session.

R

Reality

Examine the current situation without judgment.

O

Options

Brainstorm possible solutions together.

W

Will

Commit to a specific action plan.
